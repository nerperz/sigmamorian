The Purpose Of This Website:

We created this website to make it easier for Sycamore section students to stay updated without constantly checking
the announcement group chat for exam dates, assignments, and important activities. Since the group chat can
sometimes be inactive or unreliable, we thought a website would be a more efficient and dependable solution.
This platform will be regularly updated around 4-5 PM, ensuring that all essential information is easily accessible and up to date.
